**Q: Does the chatbot record my messages?**
A: No. Conversation is local only, cleared when you close the modal.

**Q: Will I get different tips?**
A: Yes. Same topic can give different tips each time.

**Q: Can I save tips?**
A: Currently no, but you can copy and paste. Save feature coming soon.

A: It provides a random helpful tip instead. Try different keywords.

A: No. For serious concerns, reach out to a school counselor.
